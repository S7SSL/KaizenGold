Who We Are

Trusted Gold Brokerage

Kaizen Gold acts as a specialist facilitator for doré and bullion gold transactions. We work in partnership with a top-tier refinery in the United Arab Emirates to deliver a seamless, secure process for our clients.

Our service centres on issuing banking instruments on a guaranteed CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight) basis, with delivery exclusively to Dubai – one of the world’s foremost gold trading hubs.

We bring together decades of industry knowledge, rigorous due diligence, and an unwavering commitment to transparency at every stage of the transaction.
