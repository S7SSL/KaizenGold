About Us

About Kaizen Gold

Kaizen Gold is a specialist precious metals brokerage facilitating doré and bullion gold transactions between sellers and a leading refinery in the United Arab Emirates. The firm operates from London, United Kingdom and Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and structures transactions on a guaranteed CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight) basis with delivery exclusively to Dubai.

What Kaizen Gold Does

Kaizen Gold acts as a facilitator — the connecting party between gold suppliers and a top-tier UAE refinery. The firm's role covers three core activities:

  • Doré gold facilitation. Arranging the sale and delivery of semi-pure doré gold sourced through verified supply chains, refined at the partner refinery to internationally recognised purity standards.
  • Bullion gold brokerage. Brokering refined gold bullion bars, fully assayed and certified, for institutional buyers and high-net-worth individuals seeking physical gold allocation.
  • Banking instruments. Coordinating the issuance of banking instruments — such as documentary letters of credit — on a guaranteed CIF basis to Dubai, with documentation that meets international trade finance standards.

How a Transaction Works

Every transaction follows a defined four-stage process:

  1. Enquiry. An initial consultation establishes requirements, volumes and transaction parameters.
  2. Due diligence. Comprehensive KYC and compliance verification is carried out on all parties before any commercial step is taken.
  3. Instruments. Banking instruments are issued on a guaranteed CIF basis, structured to the requirements of the transaction.
  4. Delivery. Secure CIF delivery to Dubai is coordinated through the established refinery partnership, followed by refinery assay and settlement.

Markets Served

Kaizen Gold works with gold suppliers, institutional buyers and high-net-worth individuals engaged in international physical gold transactions. Delivery is made exclusively to Dubai, one of the world's foremost gold trading hubs, via the firm's UAE refinery partnership.

Compliance Approach

Rigorous due diligence sits at the centre of the firm's process. Comprehensive KYC and compliance verification is performed on all parties involved in a transaction, and documentation is prepared to international trade finance standards. Kaizen Gold is committed to transparency at every stage of the transaction.

Why Dubai

Dubai is one of the largest physical gold trading centres in the world, with established refining capacity, vault infrastructure and a deep buyer market. Delivering on a CIF basis to Dubai gives both sellers and buyers a recognised, liquid settlement venue with clear customs and assay procedures.
